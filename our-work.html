Our work

Case-style stories that show how we plan treatment—not quick “miracle” claims, but clear goals, staged visits, and measurable comfort and aesthetics.

The photography below uses on-site clinical and educational imagery to match each treatment theme. Narratives are illustrative samples for education; consent-approved patient galleries are shared in consultation when appropriate.

Case study A — Alignment & shade refresh

Illustrative sample · Cosmetic focus · Multi-visit plan

What patients care about: a smile that looks natural in daylight, not “overdone,” and a timeline that respects work and travel.

  • Chief concern (sample): uneven colour and mild crowding in the social six.
  • Our approach: hygiene-first baseline, professional whitening where enamel allows, then aligner candidacy check with bite photography.
  • Value: fewer surprises—staging costs and visits so you always know the next step.

Case study B — Implant-supported chewing comfort

Illustrative sample · Restorative · Healing-aware scheduling

What patients care about: returning to confident chewing without constant worry about the gap shifting neighbouring teeth.

  • Chief concern (sample): missing molar affecting diet and confidence.
  • Our approach: 3D planning mindset (documentation + consent), healing windows explained upfront, provisional options when appropriate.
  • Value: transparent phases—surgery, integration checks, final prosthesis—so you are never guessing what “comes next.”

Case study C — Wear & bite rebuilding

Illustrative sample · Full-mouth style planning · Protect the joints

What patients care about: stopping the cycle of chipped edges and sensitivity while keeping results looking like their teeth.

  • Chief concern (sample): flattened cusps, sensitivity, and short-looking teeth when smiling.
  • Our approach: joint-friendly bite records, staged build-up where needed, material choice matched to habits (clenching, coffee, etc.).
  • Value: longevity thinking—we prioritise stable occlusion, not only front-photo aesthetics.
