About

Enamel Craft Dental Studio

A Baner practice where artistry meets clinical excellence—led by Dr. Girija Nandanwar Dhakite.

Welcome to Enamel Craft Dental Studio. We focus on cosmetic dentistry, orthodontics, and smile makeovers with an emphasis on aesthetics, precision, and your comfort—whether you need a painless root canal, implants, or routine care.

Dr. Girija is a dental surgeon with a Fellowship in Microdentistry from Government Dental College, Mumbai, and eight years of experience across cosmetic work, endodontics, and implants.

  • Warm, professional team
